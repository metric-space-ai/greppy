Greppy Qwen3.5 modification notice

The bundled Qwen3.5 model is modified from Qwen/Qwen3.5-0.8B. It is not an
unchanged copy of the upstream model or of unsloth/Qwen3.5-0.8B-MTP-GGUF.

Greppy made the following material changes:

1. Full-parameter supervised finetuning for short English function-purpose
   summaries. The historical student prompt used the `qwen35-brief-v3` chat
   template recorded in QWEN3.5-PROVENANCE.json.
2. Joint training of a next-token-prediction (MTP) draft layer.
3. Export of the merged checkpoint to BF16 GGUF.
4. Q4_K_M quantization of target and MTP weights with llama.cpp.

Current bundled GGUF SHA256:
d45e08ad7bb8787ae9b6f56b6915e8b44ac6e13c6b740fdc7bd591249209a72c

The exact recorded inputs and tool revisions are listed in
QWEN3.5-PROVENANCE.json and QWEN3.5-TRAINING-DATA-MANIFEST.json. This notice
documents modification; it does not assert that the current checkpoint has
passed Greppy's release gates.
