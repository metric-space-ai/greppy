Greppy EmbeddingGemma modification notice

The bundled embedding model is a third-party derivative of
google/embeddinggemma-300m. The publisher
cduk/embeddinggemma-300m-GGUF-with-dense-modules converted the model to GGUF,
included its dense post-processing modules, and quantized its tensor weights to
the Q4_K_M mix distributed under the filename embeddinggemma-300M-Q4_K.gguf.

Greppy downloaded that file from pinned Hugging Face revision
16eaef07700282e488368e27b992b8fe5a40c423 and did not alter its bytes.

Greppy independently reconstructed the bundled bytes from the producer's
public embeddinggemma-300M-F32.gguf artifact (file commit
6c4b6b0b86f1917506d8047478e350e51ac65fe8, SHA256
695c5960fa074ccdc7993d9a9d215a20bee20c03f8eba23111caa6de0d0c6991) with
llama.cpp revision 56fc38b9655fbe1869d8bd6cfb269418196cea69:

llama-quantize embeddinggemma-300M-F32.gguf \
  embeddinggemma-300M-Q4_K.gguf Q4_K_M 4

Two independent x86_64 runs produced the bundled file byte for byte. The
architecture is part of the recipe because the ARM64 quantizer produces a
different, deterministic byte stream for the same source weights.
