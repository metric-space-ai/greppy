Greppy EmbeddingGemma modification notice

The bundled embedding model is modified from google/embeddinggemma-300m. The
upstream model was converted to GGUF and its tensor weights were quantized to
Q4_K for Greppy's local native inference engine.

Current bundled GGUF SHA256:

The exact historical conversion command, upstream revision, and quantizer

replace this file with a reproducibly converted and newly hashed derivative
